02

How it works in Klaviyo

The complexity is deliberately low: one trigger, one split near the end, and automatic exits. Easy to build, easy to QA.

Trigger

Fires on the Placed Order metric, scoped to the products this flow is meant to replenish.

Exclusions

Active Auto-Replenish subscribers are filtered out. A re-entry guard stops a customer sitting in two instances at once.

Auto-exit on reorder

Before each send, a conditional split checks "Placed Order since starting flow." If they bought, they exit. Nobody gets chased after they convert.

The one split

A single conditional split before Email 4 on Riversol+ member status. Smart Sending on, UTMs on every link, support routed to live chat.
